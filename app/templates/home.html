LineSense

LineSense is a business tool for monitoring consumer traffic in a line. Frequently, customers will travel to a restaurant and other locations just to be met with a line that wraps around the store. Waiting in a long line is unreasonable for people who have busy schedules and can’t spare time just waiting around. If only there was a way to know how long the line is before making the trip—that’s where LineSense comes in.

With LineSense, users will be be able to view the current length of a line without ever leaving their chair. In addition, there will be a display at the end of the line to inform customers of the estimated wait time until they will be served.

LineSense works by using a camera along with object detection algorithms to identify how many customers are currently in line at any given time. LineSense will also determine the approximate wait time for customers. These numbers are stored in a database and used in prediction algorithms that approximate the current wait time. These values can then be viewed on the LineSense web application by customers and management. LineSense also comes with a stationary display that is placed in the line to inform customers of the estimated wait time from that position. Customers will be able to see if they have enough time to wait in the line without having to guess. Management can use LineSense to determine staffing and service needs during different hours of the day, and to see if improvements can be made that would help speed up the line during rushes.
